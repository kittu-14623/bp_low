BP Low - First Aid & Daily Care

User Settings

Immediate First Aid

  • Lay the person down flat and raise their legs.
  • Give water, juice, or oral rehydration solution.
  • Offer something salty like crackers or soup.
  • Loosen tight clothing and keep them calm.
  • Seek emergency help if they faint or have chest pain.

  • Include enough salt (unless restricted by a doctor).

  • Rise slowly from sitting or lying down.
  • Do light daily exercise to improve circulation.

When to See a Doctor

  • If low BP is frequent or severe.
  • If there is fainting, chest pain, or irregular heartbeat.
  • After injury, bleeding, or serious illness.
